Hoda Masteri's Bio

Hoda is a Computer Science graduate student who has taken the prerequisite and foundation courses of her program and is currently taking some Data Science courses as electives. Before moving to the United States, she was a graduate teaching assistant and her responsibilities in that capacity included tutoring students in Statics and Strength of Materials, as well as mentoring students in the Strength of Materials lab. She studied Biomechanical Engineering during her undergraduate years and was a C programming teacher assistant during her senior year. She enjoys using her knowledge to help other students succeed in school.
